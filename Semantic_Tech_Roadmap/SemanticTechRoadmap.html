ESIP Semantic Technologies Committee (STC)

Semantic Technology Roadmap


Welcome to the ESIP Semantic Technology Committee Roadmap page. If you are interestied helping to promote the use of semantic technologies, ontologies, and controlled vocabularies in the Earth sciences, please join us in developing a roadmap that provides actionable recommendations for ESIP and ESIP members for integrating semantics into their Earth Science workflows.

We seek broad community input and encourage non-ESIP members and members of other professional societies to contribute to the development of an this roadmap.
